# 👑 CROWNED
### AI Queen-Raising Simulation — PoC Demo

> *Your words shape her destiny.*

**CROWNED** is a fantasy queen-raising simulation where the player becomes a regent tasked with raising the kingdom’s next ruler.  
Through AI-driven dialogue, training choices, and rival regent interference, each playthrough creates a different queen with her own stats, tendencies, and title.

🔗 **Live Demo**  
https://muffinbiter.github.io/crowned/

## 🎮 Overview

|  |  |
|---|---|

| **Platform** | Mobile web demo (iPhone portrait, 390×844px) |
| **Setting** | Eldoria — a kingdom ruled by queens |
| **Player Role** | A newly appointed regent competing to raise the finest queen candidate |

The player selects one of three candidates and guides her growth over time.  
The candidates not chosen are raised by rival regents, who actively compete against the player and interfere with progress.

---

## ✨ Core Hooks

### 1. AI Dialogue Shapes the Queen
Your first conversation with the candidate is analyzed by AI.  
Based on how you speak to her, she begins with a different stat tendency and earns a unique title.

This makes each run feel personal from the very beginning.

### 2. Rival Regents Create Tension
Two rival regents — **Sepia** and **Ruan** — do not simply exist as background flavor.  
They actively raise other candidates, trigger interference events, and pressure the player throughout the experience.

Their presence gives the simulation a competitive structure instead of a purely single-character growth loop.

## 🌍 World Setting

**Eldoria** is a kingdom with a long tradition of queenly succession.  
As Queen Isola nears the end of her reign without a direct heir, she summons the realm’s most promising regents and orders them to raise candidates worthy of the crown.

The regent who produces the most exceptional queen will be named **Chief Regent**.

### Queen Candidates *(Demo: 3 of 6)*

|---|---|---|---|
| **Rieona** | Royal lineage | Intelligence, Charisma | Stamina |
| **Verona** | Commoner | Mental, Stamina | Intelligence |

### Rival Regents

|---|---|---|

## 🕹️ Demo Scope

This PoC focuses on validating the core gameplay loop and emotional appeal of the concept.

### Included in the demo

- **Intro flow**
  - Regent setup
  - Candidate selection
  - Persona configuration

- **AI conversation system**
  - 3–5 turns of dialogue
  - AI-based analysis
  - Initial stat direction + title reveal

- **3 weeks of progression**
  - One active training schedule per candidate
  - Locked alternatives to imply future expansion

- **3 event beats**
  - 1 random event in week 1
  - 2 rival interference events in weeks 2–3

- **Result screen**
  - Stat summary
  - Rival ranking snapshot
  - Call-to-action / future potential tease

---

## 📈 Demo Training Structure

Each candidate is given one unlocked training path in the PoC — specifically, the area she most needs to improve rather than the area she already excels in.

| Candidate | Active Schedule | Narrative Purpose |
|---|---|---|
| **Rieona** | ⚔️ Martial Training | Royal-born, but physically fragile |
| **Verona** | 📚 Academic Study | Strong and resilient, but academically underdeveloped |
| **Erion** | 👑 Etiquette Lessons | A wanderer learning how to move like royalty |

This design keeps the demo simple while still expressing each candidate’s personality arc.
